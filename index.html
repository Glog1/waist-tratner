How does it work?

Waist Training is the process of gradually reducing waist size by wearing a special corset. This practice has been known and widely popular for many centuries. Just wearing the special corset will instantly give you a beautiful, sexy shape. Extended corset wear combined with working out and eating the right healthy diet is capable of permanently removing the excess centimeters from your waist.

Destroys fat cells

Thanks to the thermal activity taking place in your body and the increased sweating, toxins and excess fat deposits will be released through the corseted area.

Kills the appetite

When you wear a corset, the compression created in the waist area will help you greatly reduce the amount of food you eat by enabling you to eat small portions more frequently instead of the standard three big portions a day.

Three rows of hooks

The classic black latex corset is also manufactured in a 3-hook style. The three rows of hooks enable you to use your corset longer as you continue losing weight – when the 2-hook corset becomes loose, you can still use the 3-hook corset with its tightest, third row of hooks.

It will fit you perfectly!

The sport latex corset is made of elastic latex which allows for unrestrained movement in both everyday life and the gym. Maximum tightening. Three-hook closure. Nine metal bones provide firmness.
